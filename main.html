RoadVision AI
FYP PROJECT
Deep Learning Road Asset Management

Automated Detection & Analysis of Road Defects

An artificial intelligence-powered smart monitoring system designed to detect, classify micro-level crack types, and analyze road surface damage in real time using a robust two-stage verification framework.

🧠 Stage 1: YOLOv8

Instantly localizes road distress instances and handles fine-grained morphological classification.

🛡️ Stage 2: EfficientNet-B0

Acts as an intelligent false-positive filter to verify structural anomalies before logging them into the repository.

🌍 GIS Mapping

Automatically projects identified pavement failures onto a geospatial coordinates layer for optimized maintenance routing.

Detected Damage Categories

LC

Longitudinal

  • • Cause: Temperature contraction and joint construction issues.
  • • Implication: Allows water infiltration, leading to sub-grade softening.
TC

Transverse

  • • Cause: Extreme daily temperature fluctuations.
  • • Implication: Loss of pavement elasticity and potential structural weakening.
AL

Alligator

  • • Cause: Repeated heavy load cycles exceeding design capacity.
  • • Implication: Critical structural failure; requires full-depth repair.
